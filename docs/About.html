Panhellenic DEI Statement
Mission Statement

​
Panhellenic’s Diversity and Inclusion Committee is committed to creating an inclusive environment for all future and current sorority women. We strive to provide information and educational opportunities to create a safer and more inclusive Panhellenic environment. As an organization at a school nationally recognized for being diverse, we hope to have a Panhellenic community that reflects the diversity seen within UC San Diego’s student body. Our team’s goal is to provide a space for women of all races, backgrounds, religions, sexualities, socioeconomic statuses, etc to feel safe and succeed.
