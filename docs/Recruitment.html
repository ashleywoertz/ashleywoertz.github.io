Recruitment
At UC San Diego, we host a values based recruitment. Our Primary Recruitment, held annually in Fall, gives women the chance to explore the opportunities of membership in all 11 of our NPC sororities. All potential new members (PNMs) are invited to learn from chapter members and leaders what makes each chapter unique, and the reality of the Panhellenic experience.
